Introduction

Being an ameteur collector beginning in 2005, I was fascinated by the diversity of the fossils that were being found. As time went on, I realized that there was a limited aumount of information to try and identify the fossils found, so in 2018 I started a project to try and help young collectore in this endeavor, and this is the result of that project. I would like it to be an ongoing project, so if anyone has photos of unlisted fossils or can identify some of the unknown specimens, please contact Bob O'Donnell at the society meetings.
-Richard Barnard
